About Me

What is Amethyst Designs?

Amethyst Designs was created by Callae Mock as a space to share her artwork as well as collaborate with others and provide opportunities to bring art into the community. Callae has a multitude of skills such as creating custom art pieces, providing customers with one of a kind or printed pieces of her work, graphic design expertise and consulting, as well as working with other artists on shows and exhibitions.

Services

  • Custom Art
  • Original Art
  • High Quality Prints
  • Graphic Design

Experience

Callae graduated from the University of Northern Colorado in 2008 with a degree in Visual Arts with a K-12 emphasis where she studied all mediums of art. She has been in many art galleries in Colorado such as the R Gallery in Boulder, The 3 Cs Gallery in Lyons, and has work on display in the city Englewood.She has also taught art at an elementary school the last 15 years as well as working towards her certification in Graphic Design.
